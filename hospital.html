The health care plan that I have through work is a good plan. Why do I need {{product_vars.hi_vars.name}}?

Even quality health care plans don't always cover all of your expenses – and hospital procedures and/or admittance can be costly. Many people struggle to pay for uncovered medical costs and are often unable to work for some time. MetLife {{product_vars.hi_vars.name}} coverage provides a lump-sum payment that may help bridge the financial gap between covered and uncovered medical expenses, and could help offset daily living expenses like a mortgage or utility bills.

Can I enroll for this coverage without having to take a medical exam?

Yes! You don't need to take any medical exams or answer any health questions. Both you and your eligible family members can enroll, provided you are actively at work.1

How do I pay for my coverage?

Premiums will be conveniently paid through payroll deduction.

Who is eligible to enroll for this coverage?

You and your eligible family members can enroll.2 You will need to be actively at work for your coverage to be effective.

What happens if my employment status changes? Can I take my coverage with me?

Yes, this coverage is portable,3 meaning you can take it with you if your employment status changes. You will need to continue to pay your premiums to keep your coverage in force. Your coverage will only end if you stop paying your premium or if your employer cancels the group policy or offers you similar coverage with a different insurance carrier.
