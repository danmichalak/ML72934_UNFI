I have a good health care plan at work. Why do I need {{product_vars.ai_vars.name}}?

Even some of the best health care plans don't always cover all of your expenses. MetLife{{product_vars.ai_vars.name}} provides a lump sum payment that may help bridge the financial gap between what is covered and what is not. It can be used to help pay for emergency room visits, unexpected surgeries, deductibles, and even daily living expenses like a mortgage or a car payment if you have lost income due to your accident.

Can I enroll for this coverage without having to take a medical exam?

Yes. You don't need to take any medical exams or answer any health questions. Both you and your eligible family members can enroll, provided you are actively at work.1

How do I pay for my coverage?

Premiums will be conveniently paid through payroll deduction.

Who is eligible to enroll for this coverage?

You and your eligible family members can enroll.2 You will need to be actively at work for your coverage to be effective.

What happens if my employment status changes? Can I take my coverage with me?

Yes, this coverage is portable.3 You can take your MetLife {{product_vars.ai_vars.name}} with you if your employment status changes. It will be important for you to continue to pay your premiums in order to keep your coverage in force. Your coverage will only end if you stop paying your premium or if your employer cancels the group policy or offers you similar coverage with a different insurance carrier.
